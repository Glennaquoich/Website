About Glennaquoich

We blend analogue warmth with modern production techniques and sounds to create music with emotional impact that grows with repeated listening. We view the studio as a collaborative and immersive space for people with something important to say.

Based in the heart of Edinburgh, Glennaquoich is keen to partner with and learn from other independent artists and labels around the world.
